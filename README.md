## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the **Digital Literacy** course to develop practical knowledge of essential digital skills required in academic and professional life.

The project was designed around the role of a **Student Digital Ambassador**, with the objective of promoting responsible, safe, and effective use of digital platforms among students.

It focuses on key areas such as:

- Digital awareness and online responsibility  
- Professional online presence  
- Coding and collaboration platforms  
- Email communication and etiquette  
- Cybercrime awareness and prevention  

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

- To understand the concept and importance of **digital literacy**
- To build a basic **professional online identity**
- To explore **coding and collaboration platforms**
- To learn **professional communication and email etiquette**
- To create awareness about **cybercrime and online safety**
