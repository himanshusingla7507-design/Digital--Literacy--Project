 # Project Overview
 
 This project was completed as part of the **Digital Literacy** course.
 It focuses on building awareness and practical understanding of digital tools,
 online professionalism, communication etiquette, collaboration platforms, and cyber safety.

The project was designed around the role of a **Student Digital Ambassador**, with
the aim of helping students understand how to use digital platforms safely,
responsibly, and effectively in academic and professional life.

# Objective of the Project

To understand the concept and importance of digital literacy
- To build a basic professional online presence
- To explore coding and collaboration platforms
- To learn proper digital communication and email etiquette
- To create awareness about cybercrime and online safety
